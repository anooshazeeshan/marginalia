# Marginalia — Product Summary

> Full-length PRD and design language documents were produced earlier in the product design process. This file is a condensed summary kept alongside the code for quick reference.

## Vision

A calm, literary, social platform where readers preserve meaningful passages from books, paired with personal reflections — closer to Pinterest, Letterboxd, and Are.na in spirit than to a reading app or a social feed.

## Core Object: The Passage

Each Passage contains:
- The quoted passage
- Book title
- Author
- Optional chapter/page
- A personal reflection explaining why it mattered
- Mood (one of a small curated set)
- Tags
- Date shared

## Version 1 Scope

- Authentication (email/password)
- Sharing Passages (create/edit/delete)
- Browsing Passages (feed)
- Searching Passages
- Personal Shelves (a user's own Passages)
- "Keep" (private bookmark)
- "This Stayed With Me" (a single, quiet reaction — not a "like")

**Explicitly out of scope for V1:** comments, followers, notifications, AI features, messaging, book clubs, document uploads/reading, any kind of document annotation.

## Brand Feeling

Designing toward one feeling: walking into a beautiful independent bookstore on a rainy afternoon. Calm, unhurried, literary, sincere — never social-media engagement mechanics (no badges, streaks, or urgency language).
