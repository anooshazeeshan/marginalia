# Marginalia — Design Principles

These principles guide every design and product decision.

---

## Reader First

Readers come before metrics.

---

## Guest First

People should experience Marginalia before creating an account.

---

## Calm Interface

No clutter.

No ads.

No endless notifications.

---

## Conversations Over Popularity

The goal is thoughtful discussion, not viral content.

---

## Books Are The Center

Books always come before people.

The reader exists because of the book.

Not the other way around.

---

## Elegant Typography

The interface should feel closer to opening a beautiful book than scrolling social media.

---

## Quality Over Quantity

One meaningful interaction is better than one hundred meaningless ones.

---

## Timeless Design

Avoid trendy UI.

Build something that still feels beautiful five years from now.

---

## Design Language (Sprint 2.5)

Marginalia should feel calm, literary, and timeless.

The interface should disappear behind the reading experience.

Every animation should be subtle.

Every page should breathe through generous whitespace.

Books—not UI—are the visual focus.
